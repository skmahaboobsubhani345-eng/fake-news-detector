How the Detection Works

STEP 01
Preprocessing
Lowercase, remove URLs, punctuation, and numbers to clean raw text.
STEP 02
TF-IDF
Convert text to 50,000-feature vector using unigrams and bigrams.
STEP 03
LR Classifier
Trained model (96% accuracy on WELFake) assigns Real/Fake probability.
STEP 04
Explanation
9 linguistic features provide interpretable supporting evidence.
Dataset: WELFake — 72,134 labeled articles. Algorithm: Logistic Regression (C=1.0), TF-IDF unigrams+bigrams, 80/20 stratified split. Achieved 96% accuracy and 99% ROC-AUC.
